# Qualify RFA

Phase: **Validation**  
Motions: **RFA**  
[!INCLUDE [Data Privacy](./includes/playbook-data-privacy.md)]  

## Overview

Customers and Account Teams can request assistance from FastTrack using
FastTrack the Orchestration Portal (FTOP). The Request for Assistance
(RFA) form is found on the FastTrack Portal.​  

## Objectives

  - ​Confirm RFA submissions to determine if the tenant meets FTC
    eligibility criteria.
  - Confirm Account Team members using tools such as Customer Account
    Lifecycle (CALC) and Premier Standard Reporting (PSR).
  - Confirm customer intent.  

## Approach

### Confirm Eligibility

Primary Role: **RFA Manager**  
Motions: **RFA**  
Applies To: **Core Onboarding**

Customers with 150+ paid seats on eligible plans across Microsoft 365
(Office and EMS) are eligible for the FastTrack benefit. This includes
education and non-profit customers for paid seats.  

### Confirm Contacts

Primary Role: **RFA Manager**  
Motions: **RFA**  
Applies To: **Core Onboarding**

Staging Contacts Tab:

  - ​Confirm Partner
  - Confirm Account Team
  - Confirm Customer  

See RFA guidance for detailed
steps: [RFA Process Guide](https://fasttrack-docs.microsoft.com/playbook/rfa-process-guide.html)  

### Confirm Intent

Primary Role: **RFA Manager**  
Motions: **RFA**  
Applies To: **Core Onboarding**

Staging Services Tab:

  - ​Confirm L1/L2 Status
  - Confirm Target Date
  - Confirm Entitlement Notes  
  - Confirm Technical Service Notes
  - Confirm Business Reason
  - Confirm Success Owner Role
  - Confirm if Data Migration is Selected

See RFA guidance for RFA
Intent: [RFA Process Guide](https://fasttrack-docs.microsoft.com/playbook/rfa-process-guide.html)  

### Update RFA Status

Primary Role: **RFA Manager**  
Motions: **RFA**  
Applies To: **Core Onboarding**

Update the status note in FTOP using the following steps:

  - ​Update Status to "Active"
  - Update Engagement Management Note
  - Update Next Action, Next Action Date, and Next Action Owner

See RFA guidance for RFA Status
update: [RFA Process Guide](https://fasttrack-docs.microsoft.com/playbook/rfa-process-guide.html)  

## Key Inputs

  - ​RFA Submission  

## Key Outputs

  - ​Update FTOP with Account Team and Partner information
  - Customer RFA qualified
